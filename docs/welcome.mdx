# Welcome

The Lighthouse Accelerator is a program designed to help social sector organizations become a lighthouse organization.

Lighthouse organizations are forerunners in the social sector, using technology and data to drive impact at scale. In particular, they have demonstrable strength in **lighthouse capabilities** that enables them to operate efficiently and effectively.

## Net Impact

Net Impact = Depth of Impact X Breadth of Coverage - Cost

## Measure -> Improve -> Scale

Organizations on the journey to becoming a lighthouse go through three phases:
1. **Measure**: Understand what impact you have, and how it is achieved.
2. **Improve**: Use data, technology, experimentation and behavioral science to grow your impact.
3. **Scale**: Increase their reach by entering new markets, and offering new products or services.

## Lighthouse Capabilities

Each of the phases mentioned above require developing and improving a set of lighthouse capabilities like product management, experimentation, marketing, and AI evals. On the left, you will find resources and guides to get you started on each of these capabilities.

:::tip What is the ideal interface for your users?

Building your own app provides the most flexibility, but it requires a lot more effort to design, build, host, and improve an app. On the flip side, WhatsApp is readily accessibly to most users, but you are constrained by the "chat" form factor. Its important to consider the tradeoffs of each option.

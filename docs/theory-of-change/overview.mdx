# Overview

The goal of every social intervention is improve some developmental outcomes. For instance, giving farmers access to better and timely advice will improve their yields.

A **theory of change** is the causal path from inputs to outcomes. For instance:

1. Better and timely advice will be made available for farmers

2. Farmers will access the advice

3. The advice will influence farmer behaviour

4. Farm yields will improve

5. Farmer incomes will rise

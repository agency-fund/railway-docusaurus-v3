# Getting Started

Building a cost-effective solution is only one part of becoming a lighthouse organization. The other part is finding a way to reach far more people than any single program, or any amount of philanthropic funding, can reach on its own. That means handing the solution to government and private partners who can carry it to a scale philanthropy alone cannot fund.

This is why Go-to-Market and Service Delivery sit at the center of Scale: they are the capabilities that let a lighthouse move from a working pilot to a solution that governments and other institutional partners adopt, pay for, and sustain, reducing reliance on philanthropic funding over time.

## Where we are

We started testing these capabilities in February 2026. What follows on the next pages is not a playbook: it's what we are learning and still testing during our first few months of discovery, marketing, sales, pricing, and service delivery work. Expect it to change as we learn more.

## What's here

- **[Go-to-Market](./go-to-market/discovery)**: Discovery, Marketing, Sales, and Pricing — the capabilities that turn a government or institutional relationship into a signed, funded contract.
- **[Service Delivery](./service-delivery)**: designing and delivering the services needed to hand a solution to a government or partner operator, while protecting what makes it work.

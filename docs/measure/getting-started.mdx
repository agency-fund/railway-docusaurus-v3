# Getting Started

Net Impact = Depth of Impact X Breadth of Coverage - Cost

## Depth of Impact

While its important for social sector orgs to report their impact metric like increase in household income, its equally important for them and their funders to understand how that impact is achieved.

- L4: Impact
- L3: Knowledge, Attitudes, and Practices (KAP)
- L2: Engagement
- L1: Tech Platform

## Breadth of Coverage

- Direct reach through programs
- Indirect reach through partners
